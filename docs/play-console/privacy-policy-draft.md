# AI Photo Cleaner Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-06

AI Photo Cleaner is a storage cleanup utility that helps you review photos and videos before deleting or compressing files.

## Information We Process

AI Photo Cleaner may request access to photos and videos on your device so it can scan for storage cleanup opportunities, such as large videos, screenshots, and visually similar photos. Media scanning is designed to support app functionality and review flows.

## Photos And Videos

The app uses Android media permissions to read metadata and media content needed to identify cleanup candidates. Your photos and videos should not be uploaded to our servers as part of the core cleanup experience. Deleting media requires your action and may require Android system confirmation.

## Analytics

The app integrates Firebase Analytics to understand product usage, diagnose funnel issues, and improve cleanup workflows. Analytics may include app events, device or app instance identifiers, feature usage, and diagnostic metadata. Analytics collection should follow the app's privacy controls and applicable consent requirements.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide media scanning and cleanup review features.
- Improve app reliability and user experience.
- Measure feature usage and conversion funnels.
- Diagnose crashes, errors, and performance issues.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal information. If analytics is enabled, Google Firebase may process analytics data as a service provider. If ads, billing, or other third-party SDKs are enabled in future builds, this policy must be updated before release.

## User Control

You can revoke photo and video permissions in Android system settings. You control which media items are selected for deletion or cleanup. Android may ask you to confirm deletion before files are removed.

## Data Security

We use platform security features and encrypted network connections where supported by service providers. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.
