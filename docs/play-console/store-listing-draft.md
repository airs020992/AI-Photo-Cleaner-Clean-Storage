## Full Description

AI Photo Cleaner helps you understand what is taking up space on your phone before you remove anything.

Review large videos, similar photos, screenshots, and other media cleanup opportunities in one focused storage cleanup flow. The app is designed for safe review first: you can inspect candidates, keep the files you care about, and only clean up after confirmation.

Key features:

- Find large videos that take up the most space.
- Review similar photos from repeated shots or moments.
- Surface screenshots and other cleanup categories.
- See recoverable space before taking action.
- Keep control of what stays and what gets removed.
- Use Android system confirmation for supported deletion flows.

AI Photo Cleaner is built for people who take a lot of photos and videos, run out of storage, and do not want a cleanup app to blindly delete memories. The app focuses on clear previews, user confirmation, and practical storage wins.
